## Overview
Communities are one of the best ways to learn because they provide discussion, collaboration, shared tools, event information, and access to current ideas in the field.

## Featured Communities

### [https://aivillage.org](AI Village)
The center of AI security hacking culture right now.

### [https://genai.owasp.org](OWASP GenAI Security Project)
Defines real risks such as prompt injection, data leakage, etc. Provides the "Top 10 risks" for AI apps.

### [https://huntr.com](Huntr)
Real-world AI/ML vulnerabilities get reported here. A good place to see what actual bugs look like.

### [https://reddit.com/r/MachineLearning](r/MachineLearning)
Mix of cuttin-edge papers + discussion. More of a research community.

### [https://www.securitybsides.com](BSides)
Local hacker community - easier entry into the security scene.
